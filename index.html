Quan Zhao (赵泉）

I am currently a postodc with the Humboldt Fellowship in the group of Prof. Dr. Harald Garcke at the Department of Mathematics, University of Regensburg, Regensburg, Germany. Before that, I was a postdoral research fellow at the Department of Mathematics, National University of Singapore (NUS). I received my Ph.D. from the Department of Mathematics in NUS in 2017, under the supervision of Prof. Weizhu Bao and Prof. Weiqing Ren. I obtained my Bachelor's degree at the Department of Mathematics in University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2013.

Email: quan.zhao(at)ur.de; quanzhao90(at)u.nus.edu;
